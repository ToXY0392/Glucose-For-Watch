# Quick start

> **Last updated:** 2026-05-23

Install Widget G7 and see your glucose on your Wear OS watch in a few minutes.

## Steps

1. **Install** the phone app (`mobile-debug.apk`) on your Android phone.
2. **Install** the watch app (`wear-debug.apk`) on your Wear OS watch.
3. **Open** Widget G7 on the phone.
4. **Accept** the legal notices.
5. **Connect** Dexcom Share (username, password, US or OUS region).
6. **Tap Sync** on the phone home screen to test.
7. **Add** the Widget G7 tile or complication on your watch.

## Dexcom requirements

- Dexcom Share enabled on your account
- Follower credentials (not the patient app login unless Share is configured)
- Works with **Dexcom G6 and G7** when Share is active — see [compatibility](../compatibility/dexcom-g6-g7.md)

## Watch tile

- Shows your latest glucose value with **standard medical colors** (green in range, yellow/orange high, red low)
- Use the **Sync button** at the bottom of the tile to request a fresh reading from the phone

## If the value does not update

1. Tap **Sync** on the phone.
2. Tap **Sync** on the watch tile.
3. Ensure Bluetooth is on and the watch is connected to the phone.
4. See [Troubleshooting](troubleshooting.md).
